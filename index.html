our services

building

We build the home or infrastructure you desire from the foundation up to the roof type ou desire. With the style, wall cladding, window types and door type designs you need with our trusted construction methods.

painting

We apply paint, stain, and coating to a wide variety of structure, including walls, inteiors, and exteriors of buildings.

plumbing

We install underground sanitary, drainage, and supply piping systems for houses and infrastructures assigned to us. We also maintain and repair pipes and fixtures associated with water distribution and sanitation systems in residential and commercial structures.

plastering

We coat walls with plaster and go over the undercoat to make sure the final coat is flawless. We design the desired texture and create ornamental plaster, recreating original designs when necessary and construct scaffolding so plaster can be applied to ceilings and other hard to reach places.

electricity

We assemble, install and wire electrical systems in new buildings. Our electricians oversee the installation and maintainance of wiring, control, and lighting systems carrying out inspections on electrical components such as transformers and circuit breakers.

paving

We offer brick paving as well as concrete paving. Our paving abilities fall within category IV, where we create pavements for cars and pedestrians only areas - private drives, pedestrian only areas, footpaths subject to very occasional vehicular overrun.
